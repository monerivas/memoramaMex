Hey guys!

We made a card game you may know as Pair we call it “Memorama” The object of the game is to discover all the matching pairs. It requires observation, concentration and a good memory to win. To start the game you need to pic one card and click on it to turn it over, remember the image on it; then choose a second card and click on it to turn it too. If the cards are not a match they are turned back over and you have to try again. If the cards match they remain face over and you have to find and other pair. You win all the pairs are face over.

Ready!!!! Let’s play!!!!
